Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Resurface and the emotional guidance process
What exactly does Resurface do? +

Resurface is your personal AI emotional coach. It offers eight coach-guided energy games based on the teachings of Abraham-Hicks, each designed to help you reach for slightly better-feeling thoughts — one at a time — until you find clarity, hope, or beyond. It's like having a wise friend who understands emotions and knows exactly how to help you feel better.

Do I need to know about Abraham-Hicks to use this? +

Not at all! The app teaches you the process as you use it. Whether you're familiar with Abraham-Hicks or have never heard of them, the emotional guidance principles work the same way. Think of it as emotional intelligence training with a proven 30+ year track record. You don't need to believe in Law of Attraction, or anything, for that matter — the practical techniques work regardless of your personal beliefs.

How is this different from other mood apps? +

Most apps just track your mood or tell you to "think positive" or "be grateful." Resurface actively helps you shift your emotional state. Instead of logging that you feel frustrated, it guides you to find thoughts you actually believe that feel better. It's coaching, not just tracking. And it never asks you to make impossible jumps from sadness to joy.

How does the AI know what to say? +

The AI coach is specifically guided in Abraham-Hicks emotional guidance principles. It understands the 22-point emotional scale and knows which thoughts are accessible from each emotional state. It's like having a coach who's studied these methods for years and can guide you through the process with wisdom and patience.

How quickly will I see results? +

You will feel shifts within minutes of using the app. That's the beauty of the incremental approach — you're not trying to force huge emotional leaps. When you find a thought that feels even slightly better, you'll feel the relief immediately.

This seems too simple or too good to be true. +

It's subtle, but powerful. Just like the 1% better concept, the real power occurs over time. Regular incremental shifts yield palpable knowledge and experience of your own control over your emotions, your thoughts, your state, your being, and the ripple effects of all those as they reverberate out from you to everything you touch.

Is this therapy or a replacement for therapy? +

Resurface is an emotional guidance tool, not therapy. It's designed to help you navigate daily emotional challenges and develop better emotional habits. If you're dealing with serious mental health issues, trauma, or clinical depression, please continue working with qualified mental health professionals. Many users find Resurface works beautifully alongside therapy.

"Law of Attraction" doesn't work. +

Perfect! Prove us wrong. You don't need to believe in Law of Attraction to benefit from better emotional management. The techniques are practical and results-oriented — if they help you feel better, that's all that matters. Think of it as emotional intelligence training rather than LOA, New Age, or spiritual. It just works.

How does the 22-point emotional scale work? +

All you need to know is that there are really just two emotions. One feels good, one feels bad. But if you are trying to feel better, it pays to get a little more specific! A list of emotion-words in front of you to pick from helps when feeling big emotions. You may not jump from anger to joy, but you can get to frustration. You may not want to stay there, but it's slightly better, and it may be all you have access to. Resurface's fully interactive scale shows you. You explore it until you can pinpoint where you are.

What platforms is Resurface available on? +

Resurface is available for iPhone and iPad on the App Store. It works beautifully on both devices with a responsive design.

How much does it cost? +

Resurface includes a free emotional check-in and Segment Intending is always free. Additional processes are available through a subscription. Check the App Store for current pricing.

Is my data private? +

Yes. All conversations are stored locally on your device. No account required, no tracking, no data sales. The only data sent externally is for AI processing, and it's not stored or associated with your identity. Your emotional work stays yours.

What if I don't feel better after using it? +

The process works when you're genuinely looking for relief. You don't have to make big leaps — just be willing to reach for a thought that feels slightly better. If Resurface isn't for you, you can request a refund through Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.
