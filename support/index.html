Frequently Asked Questions

What is Resurface?
Resurface is an AI-powered emotional guidance app based on Abraham-Hicks teachings. It helps you navigate your emotions from where you are to where you want to be, using the Emotional Guidance Scale (EGS) and personalized AI coaching.
How does Resurface work?
Resurface offers eight coach-guided emotional processes based on Abraham-Hicks teachings. You start with how you're feeling right now, and the AI coach helps guide you gradually toward better-feeling thoughts. Everything is intuitive and guided — just open a process and follow along.
Is my data private?
Yes! Your conversations are stored locally on your device. We don't have access to your personal conversations or emotional data. The only data sent externally is for AI processing, and it's not stored or associated with your identity.
Can I use Resurface offline?
Yes, Resurface has offline support. You can write messages while offline (up to 10 messages), and they'll automatically send when you reconnect. However, you'll need an internet connection to receive AI responses.
How do I export or save my conversations?
Conversation export is coming in a future update. Currently, your conversations are stored locally on your device. We recommend taking screenshots of important insights for now.
What's the difference between Resurface and therapy?
Resurface is NOT a replacement for professional mental health care. It's a personal growth tool for emotional exploration and finding better-feeling thoughts. If you're in crisis, please call 911 or 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline). Always consult professionals for medical or psychological needs.

Troubleshooting

Messages Not Sending

  • Check your internet connection
  • Look for the offline indicator in the app
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Ensure you have the latest version installed

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Force quit the app and restart
  • Check for iOS updates
  • Ensure you have at least 100MB free storage
  • Delete and reinstall the app if issues persist

AI Responses Seem Off

  • Be specific about your emotions and situation
  • Use the emotion scale to accurately indicate how you feel
  • Try rephrasing if the AI misunderstands
  • Remember the AI works best with authentic emotional expressions

Important: If you experience technical issues during an emotional crisis, please reach out to human support through traditional crisis resources.

How-To Guides

Starting Your First Conversation

  1. Open Resurface and read through the welcome message
  2. Choose a process or start with a free emotional check-in
  3. Describe your situation in your own words
  4. Follow the AI coach's guidance to find better-feeling thoughts
  5. Continue until you reach a more positive emotional state

Getting the Most from AI Coaching

  • Be specific about what's bothering you
  • Answer the coach's questions thoughtfully
  • Try the suggested better-feeling thoughts
  • Take your time — there's no rush
  • Complete the full conversation for best results
