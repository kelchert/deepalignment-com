Resurface: Deep Alignment

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18, 2025

Resurface apps are designed with your privacy in mind. No account required, no tracking, no data sales. Your emotional work stays yours.

Information We Collect

Conversation Data

  • What: Your messages, emotion selections, intensity ratings, and truth ratings
  • Why: To provide personalized AI coaching responses
  • How: Through your interactions with the app

No Account Required

  • Resurface works without creating an account
  • No email, name, or personal identifiers collected
  • No tracking or analytics

How We Use Your Information

AI Processing

  • Your messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API
  • Used solely to generate coaching responses
  • Anthropic processes data according to their privacy policy
  • No data is used for AI training without your consent

Coached sessions use AI technology provided by Anthropic. Your conversation content is processed by Anthropic's servers to generate coach responses. This content is not stored or retained by Deep Alignment LLC or Anthropic beyond the active session. All saved conversations are stored locally on your device.

Local Storage

  • All conversations stored locally on your device
  • Never uploaded to our servers
  • You control when to save or delete conversations
  • Data remains on your device unless you explicitly share it

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data
  • Share with advertisers
  • Upload to cloud servers
  • Track your usage

We ONLY:

  • Send conversation data to Anthropic for AI responses
  • Store data locally on your device

Your Rights

Full Control

  • Delete any conversation anytime
  • Clear all data through iOS Settings
  • Export your conversations (coming soon)
  • No data persists after app deletion

Data Portability

  • Your conversations belong to you
  • Export features planned for future updates

Security

Secure API Communications

  • API requests routed through our secure proxy server
  • Your API key never exposed to your device
  • Proxy only forwards requests — no data storage

Data Protection

  • API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No sensitive data stored in plain text
  • iOS sandboxing protects your local data

Children's Privacy

Resurface apps are designed for users 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17.

Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you of any material changes through app updates. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance.

Contact Us

California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under CCPA including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of data sales (though we don't sell data).

International Users

By using Resurface, you consent to data processing in the United States where Anthropic's servers are located.

By using Resurface, you agree to this privacy policy.
